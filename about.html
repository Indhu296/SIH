About FarmPortal

Digital Farm Management Portal — Smart India Hackathon 2024 Project

Our Mission

The Digital Farm Management Portal is a comprehensive web application designed to help farmers, veterinarians, and regulatory bodies monitor Maximum Residue Limits (MRL) and Antimicrobial Usage (AMU) in livestock.

Developed as part of the Smart India Hackathon 2024 initiative, this platform aims to ensure food safety, reduce antimicrobial resistance, and bring digital transformation to Indian agriculture.
